Act I — The Problem

The Challenge

The client operated in a highly competitive oil import market with no structural visibility into who dominated supply, where prices were headed, or how customs decisions were eroding their margins. Their existing reports could describe what happened, but not why — and certainly not what to do about it.

The raw data source was a fragmented dataset of 17,000+ customs declarations across multiple currencies, inconsistent country codes, and unclassified units of measure — making any analysis unreliable without a full engineering overhaul first.

Act II — The Engineering

Data Infrastructure

Before any insight could be generated, the data needed to be rebuilt from the ground up into a reliable, queryable architecture.

  • Deep Cleaning Pipeline: Engineered a robust Power Query transformation to normalize country names to ISO standards and standardize units of measure — eliminating the ambiguity that made prior reports unreliable.
  • Star Schema Modeling: Restructured the flat file into a relational Star Schema, ensuring 100% referential integrity between regional dimension tables and transactional fact tables.
  • Data Quality Logic: Implemented custom algorithms to preserve high-volume "Undisclosed Origin" records — retaining market signal that others would have discarded — while filtering genuine statistical noise.
Data Standardization & Cleaning Pipeline Act III — The Analysis

Strategic Market Overview

Designed for the Country Manager, this view answers the most critical executive question: "Who controls this market — and where is the supply actually coming from?"

  • Global Sourcing Map: Integrated Azure Maps in dark mode to visualize real-time trade routes and supply chain concentration risks by country of origin.
  • Competitor Intelligence: A dynamic ranking engine calculating live market share per player (e.g., Tenaris: $117M), enabling data-driven identification of underserved segments.
  • Context-Aware UI: DAX measures powering dynamic titles that automatically shift between Global and Country-specific views based on user filter state.
Executive Market Overview Dashboard

Financial & Customs Optimization

The second module shifts focus from market positioning to operational efficiency — specifically, how customs strategy was leaking cash that could be recovered.

  • The "Fiscal Shield" Algorithm: Developed complex DAX logic modeling "Temporary Admission" customs regimes, identifying $608k in protectable cash flow — with 37% more precision than flat-rate industry estimates.
  • Logistics Reality Check: Built a hybrid Lead Time calculation reconciling Customs Report dates against actual Arrival Dates, surfacing a critical finding: Air Freight at $6.2/kg is ~60x more expensive than Sea Freight at $0.1/kg, yet was being used as a default.
