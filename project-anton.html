High-Tech "Command Center" dashboard to visualize a $4.07 Billion import market and optimize fiscal strategy.

Role

BI Consultant

Impact

$608k Cash Flow Protected

Phase I: Data Engineering (ETL)

The raw data source was a fragmented dataset of 17,000+ customs declarations containing inconsistencies, mixed currencies, and "dirty" geographical data.

  • Deep Cleaning Pipeline: Engineered a robust Power Query transformation to normalize Country names to ISO standards and standardize Units of Measure.
  • Star Schema Modeling: Built a data model ensuring 100% integrity between Regional Masters and Transactional tables.
  • Data Quality Logic: Implemented algorithms to preserve high-volume "Undisclosed Origin" data while filtering statistical noise.
Data Standardization & Cleaning Process

Phase II: Strategic Market Overview

Designed for the Country Manager, this view answers: "Who dominates the market, and where is the supply coming from?"

  • Global Sourcing Map: Integrated Azure Maps in Dark Mode to visualize real-time trade routes and supply chain dependencies.
  • Competitor Tracking: Dynamic ranking system calculating market share for key players (e.g., Tenaris: $117M), enabling targeted sales strategies.
  • Context-Aware UI: Engineered DAX measures for dynamic titles that adapt to user selection (Global vs. Country-Specific views).
Executive Market Overview Dashboard

Phase III: Financial & Customs Optimization

Moving beyond sales, this module focuses on Operational Efficiency and Cash Flow Protection.

  • The "Fiscal Shield" Algorithm: Created complex DAX logic to calculate $608k in protected Cash Flow via "Temporary Admission" regimes, offering 37% more accuracy than flat estimates.
  • Logistics Reality Check: Engineered a hybrid Lead Time calculation (Customs Report vs. Arrival Date), revealing that Air Freight ($6.2/kg) is ~60x more expensive than Sea Freight ($0.1/kg).
Financial & Customs Optimization Dashboard
